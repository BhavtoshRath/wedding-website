Events

11th June

Haldi & Mehendi 8AM - 2PM

Turmeric, referred to as Haldi in hindi, is a significant ingredient in the pre-wedding hindu event known as the Haldi, or Pithi ceremony. Known to possess anti-inflammatory, healing, beautification, antiseptic, and purification properties, haldi is essentially a cleansing procedure for the bride and groom to be. It’s beautiful mustard yellow hue is auspicious in Indian tradition representing a life of prosperity for the couple about to begin their new life together. Haldi will be followed by Mehendi, when the bride has the red-orange mehendi "stain" applied to her palms, back of hands, and feet. Deeper the color of the bride's mehendi, the happier the bride and groom's marriage will be.

Sagai & sangeet 6PM - 12AM

Sagai, or the engagement ceremony is an important pre-wedding ritual where Radhika and Bhavtosh would come face to face and be formally betrothed to each other by their families. The ceremony would involve ring exchange and the groom’s family will give their word to the bride’s family that they will accept their daughter and will be responsible for her future well being. Sagai will be followed by Sangeet, which is a fun-filled event with music and dance performances by family and friends.

12th June

Wedding 10AM-11AM

Traditionally, the bride’s parents host the wedding ceremony, or Shaadi and the groom and his family are the guests arriving from outside to the mandap. The whole wedding ceremony sort of depicts a story of first meeting of the bride and the groom at the wedding mandap, the bride’s parents giving her away to this worthy man, called kanyadaan the couple committing to each other in front of the sacred fire followed by the couple taking the seven vows of commitment to the marriage and the friends and families blessing the newly wedded couple. The wedding will blend the traditions of Odisha (Bhavtosh's native state in eastern India) and Uttar Pradesh (Radhika's native state in northern India).

Reception 6PM-12AM

This event will mark the first public appearance of Bhavtosh & Radhika as a newly wed couple after marriage. It will be a time for both families to rejoice over their new accord. The reception ceremony would also serve as an opportunity for the couple to know the acquaintances of each other's families. This is the time when Radhika would finally represent herself as an important member of Rath family. With people mostly in western attire, everyone would be busy showing off their dance moves on the stage.
